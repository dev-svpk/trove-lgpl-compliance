# Poems for 2026-05-05

Three poems, the seventh day. Earlier days circled the work itself —
what was taken, what was returned, the document that endured, the
readers before and after. Today's poems sit with the central
instrument: the equivalence check. Two files, every name different,
the same program underneath. Today is also 端午 in Japan — Children's
Day, the day of the carp streamers. Same fish, different colours,
flown above different roofs.

---

## I. Modulo *(English)*

Two files. Every name different.
Run them through the compiler.
The bytecodes match —
allowing, as the script puts it,
*modulo identifier names and the constant pool.*

A whole word for the gap
between two ways of saying the same thing.
*Modulo:* in the only sense that matters here,
these are equal.

So much of any work, it turns out,
lives below the names —
the branches, the offsets, the calls.
The seller traded in surface.
The script measured the depth.
The depth, when measured,
came out the same.

| 中文 | English |
|---|---|
| 字易骨同判无殊， | Letters changed, bones unchanged — the verdict finds no difference; |
| 机衡千卷自相符。 | the engine weighs a thousand scrolls, and they answer to each other of themselves. |
| 表名虽换神犹固， | Though the surface names are swapped, the spirit still stands firm — |
