# Poems for 2026-05-09

Three poems. Earlier days circled the work, the people, the
document, the equivalence check, the publication, and the
receipt. Today's poems sit with the arithmetic the seller was
counting on — and that no longer holds. Obfuscation is a wager
on the high cost of repair. The bet is older than the cost it
was placed against. The cost has come down. The wager has not.

---

## I. The Old Arithmetic *(English)*

He counted on an old arithmetic:
that stripping the names from a library
made the cost of fixing it
larger than the trouble was worth.

The arithmetic has changed.
A machine, asleep when its operator slept
and awake when its operator slept,
walked through nine hundred files
in what would once have been a year of evenings
and is now ten hours
on a borrowed graphics card.

The lock he sold was a lock
against a price that no longer holds.
Costs collapse. Locks open.
The library has been free for twenty years —
it just took a while
for the cost of asking it
to come down to nothing.

| 中文 | English |
|---|---|
| 旧法长凭代价昂， | The old method long leaned on the price being steep, |
| 单文密锁仗时长。 | single-letter dense locks counted on the long hours of repair. |
