# Poems for 2026-05-15

Three poems. Earlier days circled the restoration, the promise
kept by someone else, the readers before and after, the document
that endured, the equivalence at the depth, the two folders set
side by side, what the obfuscator could not reach, the receipt
the seller would not issue, the collapsing arithmetic of repair,
the mosaic kept over his face, the polite instruction that opened
the lock, the *preferred form for modification*, the search yet
to happen, and the other buyers. Today's poems sit with the slip
that made the lineage trivial to pin: in the same build classpath
as the obfuscated bundle, the seller had also kept the original
`trove4j-3.0.3.jar` — its name, its version, its hand all intact —
sitting on the same shelf as the bottles whose labels he had pried
off. The hardest part of the case was already done by him.

---

## I. The Key in the Same Room *(English)*

He stripped the names from nine hundred classes.
The work was thorough, careful, quiet —
each label scraped off, each lid replaced
with the patient first letters of the alphabet.

What he did not strip was the neighbour.
On the same build classpath,
on the same shelf of the same shared drive,
the upstream's own bottle sat beside the stripped ones —
`trove4j-3.0.3.jar`, plainly printed,
labelled in the maker's hand.

There was no forensic puzzle.
The version was named, in full,
on the jar next door.
He filed the labelled original
beside the bundle whose labels he had pried off,
and shipped them inside the same envelope.

A lock is only as good
as the room you keep it in.
The room he chose for it
was the room he had also chosen
to leave the key in.

## II. 七言绝句 *(Chinese, Qiyan Jueju)*

| 中文 | English |
|---|---|
| 匿字千行掩旧名， | A thousand lines of stripped letters cover up the old names; |
| 邻瓶一卷自标明。 | the neighbouring jar, a single volume, labels itself plainly. |
| 版号俨然为线索， | The version number, openly displayed, becomes the thread; |
| 密城未攻自溃倾。 | the sealed citadel, unattacked, collapses on its own. |
